07

Responsive, accessibility & icons

Responsive intent
  • Desktop-first, 1440px baseline; comfortable to ~1280, usable to 1024.
  • Alignment matrix & diligence tables scroll horizontally below ~1100px — never reflow cells to cards.
  • Left nav collapses to a 66px icon rail (toggle persists); content reflows wider.
  • Cards in the decision header / activity board wrap; the workbench does not.
Accessibility (WCAG AA)
  • Caution/capacity/status always pair color with text or icon — never hue alone.
  • Body text ≥ AA contrast on white; muted greys reserved for ≥12px secondary text.
  • Visible focus ring (sky/40, 2px offset) on every interactive element.
  • Governed assertions are keyboard-operable; confirm gated on the checkbox.
Icons & assets
  • Icon set: Lucide (1.8–2.2 stroke, 13–18px). Single consistent family throughout.
  • Brand: Kroger wordmark + "K" emblem (Fresh Cart) as SVG, white on navy / blue on light.
  • No emoji. No decorative gradients beyond the recommendation-card header & login panel.
Parked, not this round: the supplier email-drafter UI is intentionally out of scope — do not design it yet. Notices generated at close-out are drafts; the draft→SENT step is a separate governed gate.
